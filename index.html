About us

Brief imformation

Amila amulet is the brainchild that Trinh Quoc Tuan is most devoted to. The reason for naming the brand name is Tinh Lam because this is a very familiar character to those who are fans of Huynh Lap in works such as "The Blind Wizard", "Who died raise your hand". Many people watching these series were interested in the accessories of the character Amila amulet, and thus Amila amulet - Feng Shui Jewelry was born.

Why should you choose us?

A successful brand is built on customer trust and satisfaction. Amila amulet was born because of that: sincerity, trust and most of all, we spend a lot of time, learning to bring the best quality products to consumers.

What do we do?

Nature always hides countless mysteries, shimmering and fanciful beauty. From the harmony of 5 elements in the universe: Eternal like Kim, sharp like Wood, glittering like Water, enchanting like Fire, mysterious like Earth. True to the name we chose, it is all about focusing on Feng Shui Jewelry. Bringing core values ​​and spirit to help people be more confident in work and life. It not only gives people external beauty, but also brings the five elements suitable for each individual to help that individual's development path more sublime.

Achievement

The strong rise has led Amila amulet to become an Enterprise ranked by the VNR 500 Rating Organization as the Top 5 Largest Private Enterprises in Vietnam for more than a decade. In addition, in the ranking of enterprises of all economic sectors, Amila amulet has continuously risen in rank and is currently ranked 14th among the 500 largest enterprises in Vietnam.
